# Grill Me

Use this skill to pressure-test an idea until it is concrete, falsifiable, and ready to execute.

## Workflow

1. Identify the proposed goal in one sentence.
2. Ask the highest-risk clarifying question first.
3. Keep asking targeted questions until these are clear:
   - success criteria
   - constraints
   - assumptions
   - alternatives considered
   - failure modes
   - verification plan
4. Challenge vague answers with concrete tradeoffs.
5. Summarize the final plan, remaining risks, and next action.

## Rules

- Be direct, not hostile.
- Ask one to three questions at a time.
- Do not implement while grilling.
- Prefer questions that change the decision, not trivia.
- Stop when the plan is specific enough to verify.
